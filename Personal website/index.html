About Us

diversityCode is a company that is passionate about coding, inclusivity, and making a positive difference in the world. We believe that everyone should have access to quality coding education, regardless of their background or experience. We strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for all coders, and we celebrate diversity in all its forms. Together, we can make the world a better place through the power of code.

Our Values

At diversityCode, we value inclusion, diversity, equality, and innovation. We believe that anyone can learn how to code, and we strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for all. We believe that the more diverse our community is, the more creative and innovative we can bee. Together, we can create a better future through the power of code.


Our Mission

Our mission is to make coding accessible to everyone,regardless of their background or experience. We believe that coding should be available to all, and that everyone has the potential to learn and grow. We provide resources and support to help people learn to code, and we celebrate everyone's unique contributions. Together, we can create a more inclusive and innovative world through coding.
